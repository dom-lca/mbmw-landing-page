How it works

Simple. Powerful. Yours.

Four steps to full control of your maternal health.

01

Create your account

Start by measuring your health level regularly via your cell phone with data that is updated in real time. So you can anticipate the worst posibility for your health.

02

Set your status

Choose your phase: trying to conceive, pregnant, postpartum, or beyond. The app calculates your week automatically.

03

Define your Availability

A personalized health plan is created automatically with mental, physical, and nutritional wellness tasks.

04

Track & grow

Daily check-ins, points, badges, and streaks keep you engaged. AI and Promotoras are always available.

Features

01
Daily health tracking
Personalized daily health and symptom check-ins for your maternity phase — weight, blood pressure, and sleep hours.

AI Chatbot
